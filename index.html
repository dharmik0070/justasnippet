Motivation - An internal desire

It is a reason for actions, willingness, and goals. Motivation is derived from the word motive, or a need that requires satisfaction. These needs, wants or desires may be acquired through influence of culture, society, lifestyle, or may be generally innate.

Inspiration - A powerful force

It makes someone want to do something or that gives someone an idea about what to do or how to act, create, or feel an emotion

Relationship - A reason to live

It is the way in which two or more people or things are connected, or the state of being connected ,the most meaningful aspects of life, and are a source of deep fulfillment.
